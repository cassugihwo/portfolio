A Little About Me

Hi! I’m Cas, a student at SFU majoring in Interactive Arts and Technology Student and minoring in Computer Science. I’m passionate about human computer interaction and making things usable and efficient for people. I aspire to build and create things that aren’t just functional but feel good too. From web development to mobile development, I have both design and technical skills. I am proficient in not just industry standard design methodologies, but also tools (such as Figma). I am proficient in programming (C++, Python, Java) and can adapt to your technical needs well.

When I’m not knee-deep in schoolwork and personal projects, I’m outside trying out a new cafe in search of The Best Iced Latte. If not outside, I’m indoors, a home brewed drip coffee by my side, playing a game that I bought on sale and will probably never finish. Though most of the time, you’ll find me curled up in bed drawing whatever’s on my mind!
